A Brief Work History

Professional Software Development Course, CodeClan Glasgow

I became interested in coding last year because I was trying to teach myself how to design websites. With my art background, I thought web desgin might be an area that I could be successful in and I was looking for something that I could do as a side business while still teaching. I bought a book on HTML and CSS and taught myself the basics. I really enjoyed them but didn't go further than what was in my book. In the summer, when I had more time to myself, I started an online course in computer science, which I also really enjoyed. When I came home from travelling I realised that I wanted a fresh, new challenge and that I wanted to dive head first into coding and go for a complete career change. I did some googling and found CodeClan. I am currently working through the pre course work and enjoying learning the command line, git and Ruby, and refreshing my knowledge of HTML and CSS. I'm especially liking the challenge of designing this website and I think I'm spending far too much time on it!
